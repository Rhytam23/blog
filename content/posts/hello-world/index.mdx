Welcome to my blog. This is the first post, and I wanted to take a moment to explain why I built this from scratch instead of using a platform like WordPress, Ghost, or Medium.

## Why Build Your Own Blog?

There are plenty of platforms that let you publish content without writing a single line of code. But building your own gives you:

- **Full control** over your content, design, and data
- **Performance** — this site loads in under 100ms because it's statically generated
- **Portability** — your posts are MDX files in a git repository, not locked in someone else's database
- **Learning** — building things is the best way to learn them

## The Tech Stack

This blog is built with:

- **Next.js 15** — React framework with App Router
- **MDX** — Markdown with JSX components
- **Tailwind CSS** — Utility-first styling
- **PostgreSQL** — For view and like counters
- **Vercel** — Hosting and deployment
